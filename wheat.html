Foods That Contain Wheat:

  1. Atta: Wheat-based, Indian flat bread
  2. Bran
  3. Bulgur
  4. Dinkle: Spelt
  5. Durum: Found in many pastas
  6. Couscous: Made from Semolina
  7. Einkorn: Marketed as corn or maize, German wheat
  8. Emmer: Another name for Durum
  9. Farina: Hot wheat cereal
  10. Farro: Spelt
  11. Fu: Made from wheat, typically Asian
  12. Graham: Crackers, crusts
  13. Kamut
  14. Matza, Matzo, Matzah: Jewish
  15. Mir: Cross between wheat and rye
  16. Semolina: Pasta, wheat
  17. Seitan: Asian, vegetarian
  18. Spelt: Often inappropriately labeled "Wheat-Free"
  19. Triticale: Wheat-Rye hybrid
